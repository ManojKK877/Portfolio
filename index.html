WORKING

POWER FLOW: ENGINE gear drive GEAR BOX chain drive SPEED REDUCTION SHAFT Chain drive ROTAVATOR chain drive gear drive CONVEYER  The potato harvesting machine is powered by four stroke petrol engine.  The four speed gear box is attached along with the engine for obtaining various speeds.  The power from the gear box reaches the speed reduction shaft for the further improvement of the torque at various speed.  The power from the speed reduction shaft reaches the rotavtor through the chain drive by means of the power the rotavator starts digs the soil and plough out the potatoes.  At last the conveyer get its power from the rotavator by the combined unit of chain drive and gear drive.  The ploughed potatoes along with the mud is guided into a conveyer cum sieving unit via plougher  The plougher consists of number of spikes which help to achieve the fine digging.  the potato is separated from the mud and husk in the sieving unit and the fresh potatoes are collected in the storage tray.
